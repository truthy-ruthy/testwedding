HIKING

Right next to our ceremony/campground is the Corona Arch hike.
Arches and Canyonlands National Park are close by and offer amazing views. Since this is a holiday weekend, we suggest heading out early in the morning or midafternoon to avoid traffic and crowds. Admission: $25/car (free with a national recreation pass). Click here to buy a mult-park pass.

MOUNTAIN BIKING

Trails in Moab tend to be rocky and challenging, so expect an adventurous day. There are a bazillion classic trails. Go here for a good overview, but we strongly recommend checking in with a local mountain bike shop about trail conditions. Good options are Poison Spider and Chile Pepper Bike Shop.
